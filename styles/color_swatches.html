üéØ Color Guidelines:

  • Focus colors: Brightest in each domain, high contrast
  • Related colors: Medium brightness, good readability
  • Other colors: Darkest, with lighter text for readability
  • Text colors: White (#ffffff) for dark backgrounds, Light grey (#eceff1) for very dark backgrounds
